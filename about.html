ABOUT US

Cepheus Payment Limited was established by the group of Fintech enthusiasts with substantial experience and expertise in asset management, money remittance, law, and international trade. The founders are willing to change the money remittance market for more flexible and human oriented approach.

OUR MISSION

We intend to provide fast and efficient remittance, and FX exchange services for end users, small- and medium-sized businesses through Fintech latest innovations. We want to make the world a better place and change people's lives through our solutions.

OUR STRATEGY

We aspire to become a global money remittance system still focusing on Hong Kong clients. We are focused on taking our services to a new level of swiftness, convenience and transparency. We see our future in establishing long term relationships with our customers based on trust, loyalty, and reciprocity.

CEPHEUS CULTURE

Our team believes that creating a company where people love to work is built on relationships, growth, and impact. We cultivate a culture of low ego and set a high bar for excellence. We’re committed to making sure diversity and inclusivity is always at the heart of our company mindset, by building a workplace where every employee feels valued as an equal.
